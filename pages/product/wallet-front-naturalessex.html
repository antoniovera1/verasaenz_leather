Exterior and Interior Leather

We use Horween®'s Essex Leather, a full-grain leather made using the same traditional vegetable tanning methods as Horween®'s famous shell cordovan. Essex is crafted from high-quality cowhide and is infused with a rich blend of natural oils during its tanning process. This meticulous process takes about 30 days to complete, resulting in a leather that is both supple and durable, with a smooth, luxurious feel. Over time, Essex leather develops a rich patina, gaining character and depth with wear. This leather is sourced from the famous Horween® Leather Company in Chicago, Illinois, renowned for producing some of the most high-quality leather products since 1905.
