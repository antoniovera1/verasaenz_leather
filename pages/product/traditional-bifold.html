Materials

We use Horween’s 2 mm Vegetable Tanned Dublin Leather. This leather is sourced from the famous Horween Leather Company in Chicago, Illinois, renowned for producing high-quality leather products since 1905. This full-grain leather is vegetable-tanned and infused with natural waxes, giving it a rich, rustic appearance that develops a beautiful patina over time, enhancing its character with age. The distinctive pull-up and natural leather grain create a unique texture that signifies luxury and authenticity.
