Contact Us

At VeraSaenz, all of our products are made to order. This means that we only begin crafting your item once the order is placed. Each piece is carefully hand-cut and hand-stitched using high-quality materials that we source specifically for your order. Due to the craftsmanship involved, please note that some of our products may take up to 4 months to complete and ship after your order is placed. We appreciate your patience and assure you that the wait is worth it for the uniqueness and quality of our products.

To order a product, please include the product name in your message. We will contact you the pricing and availability for your order.

If you would like to place an order or have any questions, feel free to reach out to us.
